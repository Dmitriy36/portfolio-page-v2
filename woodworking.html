Wood glue is extremely strong: glued properly, if you try to break the glued joint, often another part of the piece will break first. The catch is: wood cannot be wet or painted, or pressure-treated -AND- you cannot glue end-grain.

How it looks - my mistake was making it too shallow. You want it at least 12"-16" deep, or flowers won't be able to stand up straight. So, same build with 2x4 braces, but make it 2-3 pickets deep. And to avoid spending too much $ on soil, just mix it 60/40 with whatever awful clay soil this is.
